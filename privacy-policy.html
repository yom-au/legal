Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how YOM ("YOM" or "we") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use the YOM mobile application (the "App"). YOM is a tax and GPS logbook tool for Australian gig-economy workers (such as rideshare and delivery drivers).

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Policy.

1. Who we are

YOM is operated under ABN 83 665 589 909, based in Australia.

For any privacy questions or requests, contact us at: hello@yomtax.com.au

2. What information we collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide the App's tax-calculation and logbook features.

2.1 Account information

  • Your email address and password, which you provide to create and access your account.
  • Your first and last name, which you provide at sign-up.

Sign-in is by email and password only. We do not store your password in readable form; authentication is handled securely by our authentication provider (Supabase).

2.2 Financial and tax information you enter

  • Income figures (for example, rideshare, delivery, and other gig or employment income you record).
  • Expense figures (for example, fuel, insurance, tolls, phone, and other business expenses).
  • Your Australian Business Number (ABN), if you choose to enter it.
  • GST registration status and related settings you provide.

We do NOT collect or store your Tax File Number (TFN). References to "TFN income" within the App are simply a category label for employment/wage income and do not involve collecting your actual tax file number.

2.3 Vehicle and logbook information

  • Vehicle and odometer readings you enter.
  • Trip records you create, including distances and trip purpose (business or personal).

2.4 Location information

  • The App collects GPS location data only while you are actively recording a trip.
  • When you start a trip, the App records your location in the foreground and background to calculate the trip's route and distance for your logbook.
  • When you end the trip, the App stops collecting location data.
  • The App does not track your location when you are not recording a trip.

You can control location permissions at any time through your device settings. If you disable location access, the automatic trip-tracking feature will not function, but you can still enter trips manually.

2.5 Subscription information

  • If you purchase a subscription, the transaction is processed by Apple through its in-app purchase system. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not receive or store your payment card details.

3. How we use your information

We use your information only to:

  • Provide the App's core features: calculating estimated tax, GST, and BAS figures, and maintaining your trip logbook.
  • Generate reports (such as your Tax Summary and BAS report) for your own use and to share with your tax agent.
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • Manage your subscription.
  • Respond to your support requests.
  • Comply with our legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your information for advertising. We do not share your financial or location data with third parties except the service providers described below, which are necessary to operate the App.

4. The estimates we provide

The tax, GST, and BAS figures the App produces are estimates based on the information you enter and general Australian tax rules for the relevant financial year. They are not official assessments and are not tax advice. You should confirm all figures with a registered tax agent before lodging anything with the Australian Taxation Office (ATO). See our Terms of Service for more detail.

5. How your information is stored and protected

  • Your data is stored using Supabase, our database and authentication provider, on servers located in Australia (Sydney region). Your data stays in Australia.
  • Access to your data is restricted to your authenticated account.
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-party service providers

We share information only with the service providers necessary to operate the App:

Provider Purpose Data involved
Supabase Database and authentication (hosted in Australia) Account, financial, vehicle, and trip data
Apple In-app subscriptions Subscription status (no card details shared with us)

These providers process data on our behalf and are subject to their own privacy and security obligations.

7. How long we keep your information

We keep your information for as long as your account is active. Because tax records may need to be retained for several years, you remain in control of how long your data is kept — you can delete it at any time (see Section 8).

8. Your rights and choices

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct information that is inaccurate.
  • Delete your account and all associated data.

Account deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the App at any time. This permanently removes your account information, financial entries, trips, and other data from our active systems.

To make any other privacy request, contact us at hello@yomtax.com.au.

9. Children

The App is intended for adults working in the gig economy and is not directed at anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you within the App. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated Policy.

11. Complaints

If you have any concerns about how we handle your personal information, please contact us at hello@yomtax.com.au and we will do our best to resolve them. You may also contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at www.oaic.gov.au if you wish.
